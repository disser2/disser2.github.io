Foto: Anke Liepertz-Peter / Fraunhofer IAIS
I'm a Senior Data Scientist and Team Lead at Fraunhofer IAIS research institute in Sankt Augustin, Germany with 7+ years of experience. As head of the Healthcare Analytics team, I focus on research and industry projects within the areas of Artificial Intelligence in Hospitals and Pharmacology. My main scientific area of interest includes Visual Analytics and Network Science with a university background in mathematics and computer science.

Always looking for promising research & project collaborations! Just contact me, see above.
